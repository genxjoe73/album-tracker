**#1 To Infinity** is a greatest-hits compilation by Mariah Carey released in May 2015 by **Epic Records / Columbia / Legacy**. The title refers to Mariah Carey's record-breaking run of number-one singles on the Billboard Hot 100 — the album opens with her early flagship hits ("Vision of Love," "Love Takes Time") and proceeds chronologically through her career, capturing her dominance of the chart across two and a half decades. The tracklist spans her debut *Mariah Carey* (1990) through *Me. I Am Mariah... The Elusive Chanteuse* (2014), concentrating on her most commercially successful material: ballads, love songs, and upbeat pop-R&B fusions that defined her commercial identity.

The **2015 pressing** was issued on **180-gram vinyl**, a weight designation associated with collector-oriented reissues and generally implying more stable playback and quieter surfaces relative to standard 120g pressings. Columbia Records mastered this edition at their **Burbank facility**, with **CJH** (the engineer code) conducting the cut under the project name `Welcome to 1979` — a reference that may allude to the mastering session's date or artistic intent. The pressing plant is not explicitly stated on the release, but Sony's 2015 compilations were typically manufactured by either domestic or European contract presses.

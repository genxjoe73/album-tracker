**Greatest Hits** is the definitive single-disc (or double-LP) survey of The Police's catalog, originally compiled in 1992 on A&M Records. It collects the essential tracks from across their five studio albums — from the early reggae-inflected punk of "Roxanne" and "Can't Stand Losing You" through the new wave peak of "Message in a Bottle," "Every Little Thing She Does Is Magic," and "Don't Stand So Close to Me," to the massive global hits of *Synchronicity*: "Every Breath You Take," "Wrapped Around Your Finger," and "King of Pain." As a snapshot of one of the most musically sophisticated bands of the early 1980s — Sting's bass and vocals, Andy Summers' atmospheric guitar, Stewart Copeland's polyrhythmic drumming — it's nearly flawless.

The **2022 half-speed remaster** (Polydor / UMC catalog **358 717-6**) is the premium modern vinyl edition, mastered at **Abbey Road Studios, Room 30** using the half-speed process — the lacquer cut at half the playback speed, allowing the cutting head twice the time to inscribe high-frequency detail. The result is notably wider stereo imaging and improved transient response versus standard-speed cuts. Matrix codes `3587177 / BL55235` confirm this specific pressing. This is the best-sounding vinyl edition of this compilation available.

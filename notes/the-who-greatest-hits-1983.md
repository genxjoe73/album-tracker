**Greatest Hits** is a 1983 MCA Records compilation spanning The Who's career — Roger Daltrey, Pete Townshend, John Entwistle, and Keith Moon (died 1978). Covers the arc from "My Generation" (1965) through *Tommy*, *Who's Next*, *Quadrophenia*, and *It's Hard* (1982). A known catalog variant: front cover lists `MCA-37261`, back cover and vinyl carry `MCA-5408`.

This **1983 US pressing** was pressed at the **MCA pressing plant, Gloversville, New York** — identified by `KPG/MCA` in the runout (KPG = Keystone Pressing, Gloversville). Matrix `MCA 3337-W4 KPG/MCA` = Side A, fourth lacquer generation. No Apple Music equivalent for this compilation.

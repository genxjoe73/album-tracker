**Clancy** is Twenty One Pilots' seventh studio album, released in May 2024 on Fueled by Ramen / Atlantic Records — the concluding chapter of the Dema/Clancy narrative arc begun on *Trench* (2018). Debuted at #1 on the Billboard 200, continuing a run of consecutive #1 debuts since *Blurryface*. Produced primarily by Tyler Joseph; features some of the duo's most ambitious and cinematic production to date.

This **2024 worldwide pressing** was lacquer cut by **Joe Nino-Hernes at Sterling Sound** (`JN-H STERLING` in deadwax). Pressed by **GZ Media**. Black vinyl, gatefold sleeve.

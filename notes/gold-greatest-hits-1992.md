Released in September 1992, *ABBA Gold: Greatest Hits* is one of the best-selling compilation albums of all time. After ABBA effectively disbanded in 1982, their music experienced a dip in popularity, but the release of *Gold* sparked a massive global resurgence. Curated to include all of their most iconic, universally recognizable tracks, the compilation reintroduced ABBA's meticulously crafted pop to a new generation, paving the way for the *Mamma Mia!* musical and their eventual 2021 reunion.

The album serves as a masterclass in pop songwriting and production, highlighting the genius of Benny Andersson and Björn Ulvaeus, alongside the unmatched vocal harmonies of Agnetha Fältskog and Anni-Frid Lyngstad. From the disco perfection of "Dancing Queen" to the heartbreak of "The Winner Takes It All," *Gold* is an essential pop document that has remained a fixture on charts worldwide for decades.

This **2025 USA & Canada pressing** is the **retail red vinyl** edition ("RETAIL RED" hype sticker), distinct from the European black vinyl pressing. Made in Canada. Deadwax `304725E1/A 02312849` confirms the Sterling Sound lacquer.

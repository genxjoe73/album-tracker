**Born to Die** is the debut album by Lana Del Rey, released in January 2012 on **Interscope Records**. It introduces her distinctive aesthetic: cinematic arrangements, sampled orchestral strings, nostalgic melodies, and lyrics preoccupied with Americana, romance, melancholy, and mortality. Songs like "Video Games," "National Anthem," and the title track became defining works of the 2010s, establishing her as a major voice in alternative pop. The album was initially released as a major-label debut under her stage name (she had recorded earlier material under other identities), and it went platinum in multiple territories, selling over 5 million copies worldwide.

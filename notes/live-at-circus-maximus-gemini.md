Released in 2025, *Live at the Circus Maximus* captures David Gilmour's triumphant return to Rome. Following the release of his solo album *Luck and Strange*, Gilmour performed a career-spanning setlist at the historic ancient Roman chariot-racing stadium. The Blu-ray release beautifully preserves the stunning visual production and Gilmour's unmatched, soaring guitar tone in high-definition spatial audio.

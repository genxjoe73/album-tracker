**Make It Big** is Wham!'s second studio album, released in October 1984 on Columbia Records. #1 in the UK and US; three US #1 singles: "Wake Me Up Before You Go-Go," "Careless Whisper," and "Everything She Wants." 4× platinum US. Produced and arranged entirely by **George Michael**, engineered by Chris Porter. One of the defining pop records of the 1980s.

This **1984 original US pressing** was pressed at the **Columbia Records Pressing Plant, Carrollton, Georgia** — `G1` in the runout, no triangle on Side A label (the triangle variant is r13382515). Matrix `AL 39595-1C G1` = first lacquer generation, C stamper, Carrollton plant.

**Songs For A Nervous Planet** is a 2024 compilation / live-and-new collection by Tears For Fears, released on Concord Records. The album brings together four new studio recordings (recorded at Roland Orzabal's School Farm Studios, co-written with Charlton Pettus) alongside live performances of signature songs from the duo's recent touring — "Shout," "Head Over Heels," "Everybody Wants to Rule the World," and more. Issued as a 2LP gatefold with lyric insert.

This **2024 US first pressing** was mastered by **Ted Jensen** (digital) and **Justin Shturtz** (vinyl masters), with lacquer cut by **Joe Nino-Hernes** (`JN-H` in the deadwax) — all at **Sterling Sound**, New York. Pressed at **Memphis Record Pressing** (`MRP6175` stamper code). The deadwax `CRE02697-A (1) MRP6175/CRE02697-A 1987983 #4 287337E1 JN-H STERLING` confirms Side A, first stamper generation.

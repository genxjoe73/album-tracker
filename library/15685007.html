Production notes

Released March 27, 2015 by Centricity Music, How Can It Be is Lauren Daigle’s debut studio album — the record that established her as a major contemporary Christian voice before Look Up Child broke her into mainstream pop visibility three years later. First (the lead single) and the title track How Can It Be both went to #1 on Billboard’s Hot Christian Songs chart, with the album itself certified 2× Platinum by the RIAA.

The album was produced by Paul Mabury (who would also produce Look Up Child), with Jason Ingram contributing as co-writer on multiple tracks. Recorded in Nashville, the production sits in adult-contemporary worship territory — piano-and-strings ballads, mid-tempo pop-rock, Daigle’s voice (often compared to Adele’s) consistently the centerpiece. How Can It Be won Best Contemporary Christian Music Album at the 2017 Grammys — Daigle’s first Grammy, before her Look Up Child repeat in 2019.

The original 2015 release was 12 tracks. Centricity reissued the album as a Deluxe Edition in late 2015 / early 2016 with 5 additional tracks: live versions, an alternate mix, and a previously-unreleased song. The 2016 deluxe was the version that anchored the album’s continued chart presence through 2017–2018.

About this 2020 US Deluxe Edition pressing (2×LP, 180g, gatefold): Centricity Music issued the deluxe configuration on vinyl in 2020 (catno 9619210101), five years after the original digital release. 17 tracks across four sides at standard 33⅓ RPM, on 180g vinyl with a full-color gatefold jacket and custom die-cut inner sleeves. The vinyl format choice (33⅓ rather than the 45 RPM cut Centricity used for the Look Up Child LP) is appropriate to the longer total runtime — 17 tracks at 45 RPM would have required 3 LPs.

This is the more comprehensive vinyl edition of How Can It Be available — the original 12-track album was issued on standard-weight vinyl in 2015 (catno harder to find), while this 2020 deluxe is the canonical “complete” vinyl release.

Gemini Notes

Released in 2015, How Can It Be is the debut studio album by contemporary Christian music artist Lauren Daigle. The album was a massive commercial and critical success, winning a Billboard Music Award and earning a Grammy nomination.

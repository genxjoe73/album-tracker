Production notes

Boston is the debut album by the band of the same name, released on Epic Records in August 1976. It was recorded largely in guitarist and founder Tom Scholz’s basement studio in Watertown, Massachusetts, with Scholz engineering and producing most of the material himself before Epic financed formal sessions at Capitol Studios in Hollywood to satisfy label requirements. The album introduced a dense, layered guitar sound — multiple tracked guitar parts processed through Scholz’s custom-built gear — and a glossy, highly produced rock aesthetic that was unusual for the mid-1970s. “More Than a Feeling,” “Peace of Mind,” “Foreplay/Long Time,” and “Smokin’” became AOR staples. Boston went on to sell over 17 million copies in the US alone, making it one of the best-selling debut albums in American recording history.

The 2016 pressing was issued by Legacy Recordings — Columbia/Sony’s catalog reissue division — to mark the album’s 40th anniversary. It was released as a 180-gram picture disc, with the album artwork printed through the vinyl rather than applied as a paper label. Picture discs sacrifice some sonic performance relative to standard black vinyl: the absence of a uniform PVC compound and the presence of a printed layer between the two vinyl faces tends to produce higher surface noise and slightly reduced dynamic range. For a collector-oriented commemorative edition of a classic rock title, these trade-offs are generally accepted, and the visual presentation is the primary appeal.

The GA plant code in the deadwax identifies the Carrollton, Georgia pressing plant, which operated under various names and ownership structures — originally a Columbia facility, later associated with Sony and Universal Music’s manufacturing network. Carrollton was a major domestic pressing operation for Legacy and Sony catalog titles during this period. The matrix 26702.1(2) is the internal cutting number, with the (2) indicating Side 2. RE on the label is the standard reissue designation used by Legacy/Epic on commemorative pressings to distinguish them from original pressings.

About this 2016 US pressing (LP, 180g, picture disc): Legacy / Epic catalog 5355471 (barcode 889853555471), manufactured at Carrollton, Georgia (GA deadwax), matrix 26702.1. 180-gram picture disc, 40th Anniversary edition. RE reissue designation on label.

Production notes

The Police’s essential greatest hits (originally 1992), remastered at Abbey Road Studios Room 30 using the half-speed process — lacquer cut at half speed for superior high-frequency detail and stereo imaging. Double LP, 180g gatefold on Polydor/UMC (358 717-6). Matrix MILES. ABBEY ROAD ½ SPEED. ROOM 30 3587177 BL55235. One of the best-sounding Police pressings available.

Notes

Shrink wrapped with a hype sticker. Cardboard obi strip inside shrink wrap. Includes certificate of half-speed mastering. The inner sleeve reads: "2022 Vinyl Edition. Mastered and cut by Geoff Pesche at Abbey Road Studios. London." However, the record is actually mastered and cut by Miles Showell as identified by the runout matrix and the Half Speed Mastering certificate. Runouts are etched. In runouts, all Optimal plating symbols may be inverted (e.g., “⊥” vs “T”) and/or mirrored (e.g., “1△” vs “△↾”). Made in EU on labels. Some copies have "Made in Germany" sticker on shrink wrap.

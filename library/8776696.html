Production notes

Notes

Rehearsed at Southcombe Studios, Burbank. Recorded at the Village Recorder, Los Angeles. Mixed at Crystal Sound's Studio 'B', Los Angeles. Engineered by Peter Henderson of Air London. All songs © 1979 Almo Music Corp. and Delicate Music (ASCAP) ©℗ 1979 A&M Records, Inc. This inner sleeve differs from some inner sleeves in that Side 1 has "AMLK 63708" and the A&M logo on the top left corner, there are copyrights on songs at the end of each lyric, so there is no space between the lyrics and the title of the next song, and Side 2 has printing all the way down the right side of the sleeve, with no large gaps.

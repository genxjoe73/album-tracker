Wish You Were Here -50-

Production notes

Released in September 1975, Wish You Were Here followed the massive success of The Dark Side of the Moon. The album deals with themes of absence, the music business, and former band member Syd Barrett. The recording sessions were notoriously difficult, but the result is a deeply cohesive and emotional record that many band members consider their favorite.

Legacy (The Greatest Hits Collection)

Production notes

Legacy: The Greatest Hits Collection is a retrospective compilation of Boyz II Men released in October 2001 on Motown Records. It charts the group’s career from their 1991 debut Cooleyhighharmony through the early 2000s, spanning ten years and six studio albums of material. The selection emphasizes their signature ballads and chart-topping singles — “Motownphilly,” “End of the Road,” “I’ll Make Love to You,” “On Bended Knee,” and others — representing the core of their commercial identity. The compilation was updated and reissued several times; this 2020 pressing is a UMe Limited Edition version.

Pallas is a German vinyl pressing facility based in Hanover and is consistently regarded as one of the highest-quality pressing plants in Europe. Pallas pressings are known for quiet vinyl, excellent center-hole precision, and careful QC. The plant code 56266 appears on all pressings handled by Pallas and is a hallmark of their work. For contemporary compilations of popular catalog material, a Pallas pressing is a favorable configuration.

The purple translucent vinyl is a visual distinction specific to this 2020 limited edition. While colored vinyl typically has marginally higher surface noise than black vinyl due to variations in compound uniformity, purple translucent is generally among the more stable colored variants and does not usually carry a significant penalty relative to other colors.

About this 2020 US pressing (2×LP, purple translucent vinyl): Motown / UMe Limited Edition, catalog B0032890-01 (barcode 602435161211), manufactured by Schallplattenfabrik Pallas GmbH, Hanover, Germany (plant code 56266). Purple translucent vinyl, 2×LP, limited edition reissue of the 2001 compilation.

Make It Big

Production notes

Make It Big is Wham!’s second studio album, released in October 1984 on Columbia Records (US), and the record that made George Michael and Andrew Ridgeley international superstars. It reached #1 on both the UK Albums Chart and the Billboard 200, produced three US #1 singles — “Wake Me Up Before You Go-Go,” “Careless Whisper,” and “Everything She Wants” — and is certified 4× platinum in the US. Produced and arranged entirely by George Michael (with engineering by Chris Porter), the album is a showcase for Michael’s remarkable pop songwriting instincts and his ability to channel American soul, R&B, and funk through a distinctly British lens.

The album arrived at the peak of the mid-80s pop boom and remains one of the defining records of its era — ebullient, impeccably crafted, and held together by Michael’s vocal performances, which were already operating at a level beyond most of his contemporaries. The inclusion of “Careless Whisper” (technically a George Michael solo single in the US, but sequenced on the album) gives it a rare emotional range — from the carefree euphoria of “Wake Me Up” to the adult melancholy of “Careless Whisper” and the pointed social commentary of “Everything She Wants.”

This 1984 original US pressing was pressed at the Columbia Records Pressing Plant in Carrollton, Georgia — identified by G1 in the runout (no triangle on the Side A label, distinguishing it from the variant at r13382515). The matrix AL 39595-1C G1 identifies Side A as first-lacquer-generation (1), C stamper, Carrollton plant.

About this 1984 US pressing (Vinyl, LP): Columbia catalog FC 39595, original 1984 US pressing. Carrollton, GA pressing plant. Matrix AL 39595-1C G1 (Side A). Barcode 07464395951.

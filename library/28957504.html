Production notes

Greatest Hits is Shania Twain’s career-spanning compilation, originally released in November 2004 on Mercury Nashville / UMG. Spanning her three landmark studio albums — The Woman in Me (1995), Come On Over (1997), and Up! (2002) — it collects the songs that made Twain the best-selling female country artist of all time and one of the best-selling artists across all genres, with over 100 million records sold worldwide. Produced primarily by her then-husband Robert John “Mutt” Lange, the albums these songs come from set new commercial and production standards for country-pop crossover, with Twain’s distinctive blend of country roots, rock edge, and pop songcraft opening country music to a global mainstream audience.

The compilation’s 23 tracks include defining hits: “Man! I Feel Like a Woman!”, “You’re Still the One”, “That Don’t Impress Me Much”, “From This Moment On”, “Come On Over”, and “Any Man of Mine” — a run of singles that dominated both country and pop charts through the late 1990s and early 2000s. The 2023 remaster revisits the original recordings with updated sonic presentation.

This 2023 US pressing is the limited “Coke Bottle Clear” green vinyl edition, a Walmart/Target exclusive 2LP released in a gatefold sleeve with printed inner sleeves. Pressed by GZ Media — confirmed by the 10-101587 / 602458342178 deadwax lacquer code, UMe’s standard GZ pressing format for this era.

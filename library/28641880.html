Production notes

Katy Perry’s commercial peak — the first album by a female artist to produce five Billboard Hot 100 number-one singles (“California Gurls,” “Teenage Dream,” “Firework,” “E.T.,” “Last Friday Night”), tying Michael Jackson’s Bad. Max Martin and Dr. Luke’s production is pristine pop architecture. 2023 reissue on Capitol/UMe (B003807801) pressed in Czech Republic. Cotton candy pink vinyl. Mastered by Brian Gardner.

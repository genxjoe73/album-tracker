Production notes

Live at the Circus Maximus is a concert film and recording by David Gilmour, capturing a solo performance at the Circus Maximus in Rome in 2024. Gilmour, the primary guitarist and one of the surviving principal members of Pink Floyd, has maintained an active solo touring schedule since the early 1990s, balancing original material with carefully selected Pink Floyd repertoire. The Circus Maximus venue — an ancient Roman amphitheater — is one of the world’s most storied concert destinations, and its use for this performance signals the gravitas and scale of Gilmour’s touring operation.

The concert showcases Gilmour’s signature guitar-centric approach to both his original compositions and the Pink Floyd material he’s chosen to carry forward in his solo context. His touring band typically includes longtime collaborators and brings a distinct sonic identity to these performances — less symphonic than some Pink Floyd arrangements, more focused on Gilmour’s distinctive guitar work and his evolution as a solo artist.

The 2025 release is issued by Sony Music Entertainment under exclusive license from David Gilmour Music Ltd., suggesting a formal partnership between Gilmour’s independent label operation and Sony’s distribution and marketing infrastructure. The Blu-ray format delivers the performance in high-resolution video and audio, allowing for the dynamic range and spatial presentation befitting Gilmour’s carefully crafted sonic aesthetic. Made in Germany indicates pressing plant origin, likely reflecting European manufacturing and distribution logistics.

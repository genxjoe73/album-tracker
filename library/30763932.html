Production notes

Clancy is Twenty One Pilots’ seventh studio album, released in May 2024 on Fueled by Ramen / Atlantic Records, and the concluding chapter of the narrative arc the duo — Tyler Joseph and Josh Dun — began with Jumpsuit on Trench (2018). The album resolves the fictional mythology of “Dema” and the character Clancy that has threaded through their recent work, delivered with the band’s characteristic genre-blending of alternative rock, synth-pop, hip-hop, and arena anthemics. It debuted at #1 on the Billboard 200, continuing a run of consecutive #1 debuts that began with Blurryface.

The album was produced primarily by Tyler Joseph and features some of the duo’s most ambitious production work to date — dense layering, cinematic dynamics, and a confident command of their sonic identity that nine years of arena touring has sharpened considerably. Tracks like “Overcompensate,” “Paladin Strait,” and “Navigating” balance accessibility with genuine emotional weight.

This 2024 worldwide pressing was lacquer cut by Joe Nino-Hernes at Sterling Sound, New York — the JN-H STERLING in the deadwax confirms both the cutter and facility. Pressed by GZ Media. Issued in a gatefold sleeve, black vinyl.
